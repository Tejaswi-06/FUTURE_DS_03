## 🧾 Project Overview
This project presents an **interactive Power BI dashboard** that analyzes the end-to-end marketing funnel.  
It helps track how users move from impressions to conversions and evaluates campaign performance across multiple channels.

The dashboard is designed to support **data-driven marketing decisions** by visualizing key metrics and trends.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives
- Analyze the complete marketing funnel performance  
- Measure campaign effectiveness across channels  
- Track customer acquisition and conversion rates  
- Optimize marketing spend and ROI  

---

## 📌 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- **Total Impressions:** 28M  
- **Total Clicks:** 2M  
- **Total Leads:** 570K  
- **Total Customers:** 92K  
- **CTR (Click Through Rate):** 0.07  
- **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost):** 172.92  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Insights

### 🔹 Funnel Stage Analysis
- Visual representation of user drop-off at each stage:
  - Leads → Qualified Leads → Opportunities → Customers  
- Helps identify bottlenecks in conversion

### 🔹 Channel Performance
- Customer acquisition by:
  - Instagram Ads  
  - Facebook Ads  
  - Google Ads  
  - LinkedIn Ads  
  - Email Marketing  

### 🔹 Monthly Trends
- Tracks customer growth over time  
- Identifies seasonal or campaign-based fluctuations  

### 🔹 Campaign Performance
- Lead generation by campaign type:
  - Lead Gen  
  - Discount Campaigns  
  - Brand Campaigns  
  - Seasonal Campaigns  

### 🔹 Spend Analysis
- Distribution of total marketing spend across channels  
- Helps evaluate cost efficiency  

### 🔹 Leads vs Spend Trend
- Compares generated leads with marketing investment  
- Useful for ROI analysis
